About us

Escape normality and enjoy a retreat in one of our luxorious staycations across the beautiful west Wales coast. ‘Tŷ’ is Welsh for home, and that’s where we want our sites to feel like. We offer lots of places to stay including cosy log cabins, glamping yurts, friendly cottages and romantic hideaways with hot tubs and we’re nested in Welsh towns where you can submerge yourself in local culture, the sound of the sea and mountainous views with some quality time alone or with loved ones. We will provide you all of the comfort and care of a 5-star hotel in a cosy home with no detail spared.

Our Locations
